##  List of Flowcharts (13)

1. Flowchart 1 – *Max of 2 Numbers*:find  the maximum number from given 2 numbers
2. Flowchart 2 – *Max of 3 Numbers*:find the maximum number from given 3 numbers
3. Flowchart 3 – *Max of 4 Numbers*:find the maximum number from given 4 numbers
4. Flowchart 4 – *Sum of N natural number*
5. Flowchart 5 – *Factorial of N numbers*
6. Flowchart 6 –Print pattern::**1 -2 3 -4,.. upto N**
7. Flowchart 7 – Print pattern::**1 1 2 1 2 3 ,..**
8. Flowchart 8 – Print pattern::**1! -3! 5! -7!,...**
9. Flowchart 9 – Print pattern:**1 1 1 3 2 5 3 7 5 9 8 11 13 13 ,for N=7**
10. Flowchart 10 – Print pattern:: **1 1 2 1 1 2 3 2 1,...N=3**
11. Flowchart 11 – Print pattern::** 1 2 3 2 3 4 5 4 3 ,...N=3**
12. Flowchart 12 –**Print pattern:: 1,..6 7 8 9 0 1 0 9 8 7 6 ,N=6**
13. Flowchart 13 – Print pattern(**Taylor Series(sine)**)::**X - (X^3)/3! +(X^5)/5! - (X^7)/7!   here X=2 ,N=4**
